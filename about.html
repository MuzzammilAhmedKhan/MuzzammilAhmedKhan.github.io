Our History

Founded in 1952 by Mr. Rajesh Bharti with an initial investment of ₹5 million.

Early focus on supplying steel to local construction projects.

Expansion in the 1980s to serve national infrastructure development.

Modernization of production facilities in the 2000s increased output by 60%.

Currently producing 500,000 metric tons of steel annually.
