Products

  • TMT Bars: High-strength, earthquake-resistant TMT bars (Fe 500, Fe 550 grades) conforming to IS 1786:2008 standards.
  • Structural Steel: Wide range of structural steel products, including angles, channels, beams, and flats meeting IS 2062:2011 standards.
  • Wire Rods: High-quality wire rods for various applications including fasteners, welding electrodes, and cable manufacturing according to IS 6723:1972.
  • Custom Steel Solutions: Tailored steel products based on specific client needs and specifications.

Manufacturing Process

  • Raw material sourcing from trusted suppliers of iron ore, coal, and limestone
  • Ironmaking in blast furnaces with a capacity of 1,500 metric tons per day
  • Steelmaking using basic oxygen furnace (BOF) technology with 99.5% efficiency in removing impurities
  • Continuous casting process for producing high-quality billets and blooms
  • Rolling mill operations for shaping steel into desired product forms

Quality Control

  • Stringent quality control measures at every stage of production
  • Spectrometric analysis for precise chemical composition control
  • Ultrasonic testing for detecting internal flaws and discontinuities
  • Mechanical testing to ensure desired strength, ductility, and hardness
  • Third-party inspection by Bureau Veritas and SGS available upon request
